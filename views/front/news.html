WIMA Members' Day Event 2020

11 March 2020

On the 26th of February, Worldwide Industrial & Marine Association (WIMA) organized for the first time an event especially dedicated to its members.

The event took place on board the cruise ship “Celestyal Crystal” and we had the chance to discuss with the rest of the Companies-Members about our cooperation until now and our plans for the future.

We are proud to announce that our Company received the award of “WIMA Most Valuable Member”
in recognition of its overall contribution and solidarity.
The award was received by Mr Dimitrios Gogos, Director of Go Shipping, who during his speech, thanked WIMA for the honor and also WIMA's members for their decisive contribution to the projects received so far.

We look forward to more synergies among WIMA’s members and we will do our best to make them happen!
