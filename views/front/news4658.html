Our latest news & announcements

SMM 2016 Exhbition in Hamburg

26 September 2016

Further to our long standing presence in all major international maritime events, Go Shipping & Management Inc., participated in the SMM Hamburg 2016 Exhibition which took place in Hamburg between the 6th-9th of September 2016 in the first ever Greek pavilion, in a joint venture with W.I.M.A.

Posidonia 2016 Exhibition in Athens

13 June 2016

Go Shipping & Management Inc. proudly participated in one of the world’s most prestigious maritime event, Posidonia 2016, which took place in Athens between the 6th-10th of June 2016. Under W.I.M.A's umbrella, we managed to share our common interest in the shipping sector with our partners and make new friends in the industry.

Europort 2015 Exhibition in Turkey

6 March 2015

Further to our continuous presence over the last years in the greatest international maritime events and exhibitions, Go Shipping & Management Inc. is proud to announce its participation as a member of WIMA (Worldwide Industrial Marine Association) in Europort Istanbul 2015. Exposhipping – Europort Istanbul, 13th International Maritime Exhibition is the leading maritime exhibition of the Turkish Maritime Sector and one of the largest in the region, and will take place from March 11-14, 2015, in Istanbul, Turkey.

 
We will be pleased to welcome you in Stand No J30 (Hall 10) in a joint venture with HEMEXPO and share our keen interest on the shipping sector. 

Looking forward to meeting you in person!

SMM 2014 Exhibition in Hamburg

11 August 2014

Go Shipping & Management Inc. proudly announces its participation in the SMM 2014 Exhibition, being regarded as the number one stimulus event of the maritime industry, taking place between 9-12 of September, 2014, in Hamburg, Germany.

Looking forward to welcoming you in our Stand No. 103 (Hall B3) in a joint venture with HE.MA.CO (Hellenic Maritime Companies) and share our keen interest on the shipping sector.
